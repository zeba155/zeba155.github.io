Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g minced beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened.

3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.

4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 15 minutes.

5. Drain the spaghetti and toss with the sauce.

6. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Chinese Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat sesame oil in a wok over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.

2. Add the peas and carrots, cooking until they are tender.

3. Push the vegetables to the side and pour in the beaten eggs, scrambling them in the pan.

4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, stirring everything together.

5. Stir-fry for 5 minutes, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, garam masala, and turmeric for at least 1 hour.

2. In a large pan, melt the butter and sauté the onions and garlic until softened.

3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.

4. Stir in the tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.

5. Pour in the cream, stir well, and cook for another 5 minutes.

6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
